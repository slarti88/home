About Me

I strive to make games that provide meaning

I am a solo indie game developer from Bangalore India. I've worked on and released seven indie games over the past few years for PC and mobile. I've worn many different hats as an indie game developer but my primary skill is programming and game-design. Before game development I was a product architect at a tech-startup making applications for Android phones.

  • Made and published 7 indie games
  • Won awards for our first game The Light Inside Us
  • Proficient in Unity, C#, and C++
  • Worked on a variety of genres, from puzzles to simulation and arcade-action.

Game Design
